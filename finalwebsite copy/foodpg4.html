Black and Bleu Burger




Black and Bleu burgers are my #4 favorite food. Whenever I don't want a common burger with cheese and pickles, the black and bleu burger is my go-to.

They get their name from the dark spices that are used (black) and all the bleu cheese put on top of them.

The best black and bleu burger I've had is at Liberty Tap Room and Grill in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina.

Black and Bleu burgers are very easy to make. You just put a mixture of your favorite spices on the burger along with bleu cheese. Then you put even more bleu cheese on the melted cheese.

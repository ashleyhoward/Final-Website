Sushi



Sushi is my #1 favorite food. I eat it about once a month and I could easily eat it every day for the rest of my life. My favorite ingredients in sushi are eel, crab, raw tuna or salmon, cream cheese, and avocado.

My favorite restaurants to get sushi are Morimoto in Philly and Elena Wu in Voorhees, NJ.

For people who haven't tried sushi, I would recommend a California roll or a spicy tuna roll.

For the experienced sushi lovers, I recommend trying special rolls with a lot of ingredients.

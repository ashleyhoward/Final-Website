Shrimp and Gritz



Shrimp and Gritz is my #3 favorite food. I prefer my shrimp and gritz cheesy with some vegetables in them.

Shrimp and gritz is a southern dish, so they're mainly served down south and that's where they're the best.

The best shrimp and gritz I've ever eaten were in Charleston, South Carolina at Coast restaurant.

If you don't like shrimp, I recommend getting gritz as a side dish when down South.

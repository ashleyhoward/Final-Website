Eggplant Parm



Eggplant Parm is my #5 favorite food. Whenever I get Italian food, this is my favorite meal to get.

My favorite restaurant to get eggplant parm is Buca di Beppo.

Eggplant parm is a great vegetarian meal; if you don't like eggplant chicken parm is a great alternative.

Eggplant parm is very easy to make on your own; you just fry some eggplant and put mozzerella cheese and sauce on it.

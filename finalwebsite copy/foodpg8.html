Mac & Cheese



Mac & Cheese is my #8 favorite food. This is a great comfort food that's sure to make anyone happy.

My favorite restaurant to get this at is Panera Bread, but my mom's recipe is definitely my favorite.

Mac & Cheese can be made with a cheese sauce or baked. I don't have a preference because they're both delicious.

If you want a healthy option for Mac & Cheese, you can sub the pasta out with cauliflower.

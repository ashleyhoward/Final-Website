Gyros



Gyros are my #6 favorite food. Gyros are meat with things like onions and tomatoes on a flatbread.

Gyros are a Greek dish and can be found at Mediterranean or Greek restaurants.

The best gyro I've ever had was at the Mediterranean Deli in Chapel Hill, North Carolina.

If you want to try an authentic or adventurous gyro, try it with lamb.

Spinich Pasta with Shrimp



Spinich pasta with shrimp is my #7 favorite food. This is not only delicious, but also a healthy pasta option.

My favorite restaurant to get this at is Maggiano's but my mom makes the best by far.

The best thing about this meal is that the spinich in the pasta is healthy but you can't even taste it.

If you don't like seafood, you can get this meal with chicken or just the plain pasta.

Crab Cakes



Crab cakes are my #2 favorite food. I prefer broiled crab cakes over fried and I love eating them with either cocktail or tarar sauce.

My favorite restaurant to get crab cakes is the Olive Tree in Aberdeen, Maryland. Some family friends who live in Maryland and have been to all the best places took us there and they were the best I've ever had.

If you don't really like seafood, I would recommend finding a crabcake with more filling than crab meat.

Blueberry Pie



Let's talk about dessert now. Blueberry pies are one of my favorite deserts, especially when they're crumb pies.

My favorite place to get a blueberry pie is Red Eagle market in NJ.

Blueberry pies not only taste good but the color of the blueberries is really pretty.

Blueberry pies have fruit so they're a lot healthier than cakes and other desserts which is a plus!
